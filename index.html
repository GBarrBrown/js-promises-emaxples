What is a promise?

"A promise is an object representing the eventual completion or failure of an operation"

An object can be in one of three states: Pending, Resolved or Rejected




Why use promises?

Promises are non-blocking, meaning that while a promise is in its pending state other code can still execute (even other promises)

This can be demonstrated by starting a 5 second wait, then immediately starting a 1 second wait

The 5 second wait is still going to come back with it's confirmation once after 5 seconds, but in the mean time we are free to call a 1 second wait
